About Us

Our History

DonamiSport, a 501(c)3 organization, was founded by Doc Lawson in 2010. Traumatized by 20 years of civil war, the country of Liberia has come to recognize its own YMCA in the non-traditional role of a social services provider. DonamiSport, in partnership with the over 100 year-old YMCA of Liberia, will be of tremendous support to this organization – heavily involved in implementing psychosocial programs (health information services, psychological counseling, non-violent conflict resolution, etc.) DonamiSport will provide a sports program initiative that will support the psychosocial programs.

Currently, there is no existing infrastructure throughout Liberia for community-based sports programs for children ages 4-14. DonamiSport will provide the resources and expertise to create that plus a teen sports initiative for 15-17 year olds. DonamiSport will be the “third place”* community-based social network for young people and additionally serve as the feeder system to support the Liberia YMCA’s already-existing programs.

DonamiSport is currently partnering with the YMCA of Liberia, West Africa to create a sports infrastructure for the children of the country. This includes organizing sports leagues, upgrading facilities, creating a student athletes academy, acquiring sports equipment for the children and procuring funding.

Leadership

Doc Lawson Founder

DonamiSport, a 501(c)3 organization, was founded by Doc Lawson, honored Olympic and professional athlete. Mr. Lawson is a youth sports consultant who designs sports infrastructures for youth. He is a charismatic visionary focused on inclusion and who is well-networked with successful entrepreneurs, investors and corporate leaders. With a reputation for excellence, he is strongly committed to the educational and athletic development of kids. His orientation is international and his dedication to global social change is through youth sports. Mr. Lawson served 14 years on the Board of Directors of the United States Soccer Federation. Born in Liberia, West Africa, he returned for the first time in 40 years in December 2009. Has made a commitment to help the children of Liberia by building a national youth sports infrastructure.

Trudy Heatherly US Operations Director

A recreational soccer player who joined Doc in his mission to serve the children of Liberia soon after retiring from Verizon. She applies her organizational leadership skills to serve as the primary focal point in coordinating the delivery or pick up of donations as well as orchestrating the involvement of hundreds of volunteers to help sort and pack all donations received each year. She works with the shipping companies and Customs Inspection team to ensure smooth sailing for our container as it heads to Liberia.

Doug Heatherly US Operations Site Manager

Doug brings tremendous discipline and operational efficiency to the multi-faceted process of securing donations, packing, preparing for inspection and transferring for shipment. He ensures the storage facility is maintained in tip-top shape.
