Donate

Sports Equipment and Clothing Donations

DonamiSport primarily accepts soccer equipement and soccer clothing; however, it also accepts other types of sports equipment and clothing including non-sports clothing. In addition, on occassion, DonamiSports accepts furniture and other items. Non-sports related items need to be approved by leadership before shipping or transport to the Carrolton packing facility.

Donations can be dropped off at the Carrolton facility with pre-arranged dropoff times. The office is not always staffed during the week so pre-arrangements for dropoff date and time need to be scheduled in advance.

Arrangements can also be made to pickup donations at the donator's location upon request. Depending on the quantity and type of donations - pickups may be scheduled to allow multiple locations to be picked up the same day. Especially if the quantity requires larger capacity space such as a small or medium size cargo van or truck.

Please use our email link below to request that we contact you to make arrangements for your donation.
